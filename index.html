BE - SOBRE NÓS

BLOCKCHAIN EKOSYSTEM is a publicly traded subsidiary company formed by a network of blockchain participants, sharing business goals and processes.
Blockchain EKOSYSTEM is an ecosystem that is divided into various branches of activities using Blockchain technology and Web 3.0 mechanisms. Its aim is to harness the potential of decentralized technologies to change the predominant use of fiat currencies, introducing cryptocurrencies which, in turn, are more transparent, efficient and secure.

  • Consulting across the Blockchain industry.
  • Management through the Blockchain industry.
  • Innovation through the Blockchain Industry.
